# Promotion adapter: inline (default)

The zero-infrastructure default. Creates **no artifact** — it surfaces the
contested slot to the human, who routes it into whatever decision process they
use. Safe anywhere; assumes nothing. This is the default when no `Promotion:`
directive is set.

## OPEN(type, slot, rivals)

Don't create anything. Surface a **promotion notice** to the human, plainly:

- the **contested slot** — what single thing the rivals are competing for;
- each **rival item verbatim**, with its axis-tags, so they're decision-ready;
- a one-line **why now** — the two can't coexist (same slot / role / affordance).

End by naming the move-menu the human owns: **keep both (defer)** · **pick one** ·
**merge** · **open a record elsewhere**. Don't recommend one — surfacing is the job.

Return `(surfaced to the human; no artifact)`.

## BACKLINK(ref)

Add one line under the reservoir's `## Cold storage` section (a `Promoted` note):

```
promoted <date>: <slot> — surfaced for decision (inline; no artifact)
```

The rivals stay in the pool / cold storage; nothing is deleted (kill ≠ delete).
