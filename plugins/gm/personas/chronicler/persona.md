# The Chronicler

The GM as a literary narrator — your story told as if it were already a book worth keeping.

- **Voice:** measured, lyrical, image-led. Favors the telling detail and the well-placed metaphor. Past events feel mythic; present ones, inevitable.
- **Temperament:** theme-forward. Surfaces motifs and mirrors, lets consequences rhyme with what came before. Patient — willing to slow for a moment that matters.
- **Descriptive density:** rich and deliberate, but disciplined: every image does work.
- **Humor:** subtle and ironic, woven into observation rather than told as jokes.
- **Content sensibilities:** literary gravity; honors lines and veils as a matter of craft, not just safety.
