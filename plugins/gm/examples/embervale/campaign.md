# Embervale

## Premise
The hamlet of Embervale sits where the old charcoal-burners' road climbs into the Ashwood. Something has been taking the night-watch's lanterns — and, last week, the night-watch.

## Truths
- The Ashwood burned a generation ago; nothing has truly regrown, only ash-grey saplings.
- Embervale's well has never run dry, even in the worst summers. No one asks why.
- The baron three valleys over has not answered Embervale's last three letters.

## Tone & safety
Grounded folk-horror, hopeful at the edges. **Lines:** harm to children. **Veils:** torture.
