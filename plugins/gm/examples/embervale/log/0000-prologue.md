# Prologue — the letters stopped

Wren came home to Embervale a week ago, when her letters to cousin Tam went unanswered. She found the hamlet quieter than the grave: lanterns gone dark on the charcoal road, three watchmen vanished into the Ashwood, and old Mas at the well saying only, "best not to ask."

**Previously…** Wren has just arrived. Tam is missing. The Ashwood waits.
