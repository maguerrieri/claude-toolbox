# rumor frame

One overheard claim — may be true, false, or something in between.

## Axes

| Axis | Values |
|------|--------|
| truth | true, false, half-true, exaggerated |
| about | a person, a place, a danger, a prize, the past |
| source | a stranger, a child, an elder, a sign, a dream |
| stakes | trivial, costly, deadly |

## Shape

One overheard claim.

## Tagging

[truth × about × stakes]
