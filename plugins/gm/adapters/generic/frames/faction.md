# faction frame

A group that wants something and can act on it.

## Axes

| Axis | Values |
|------|--------|
| power | martial, economic, faith, knowledge, crime |
| goal | expand, endure, purify, profit, avenge |
| method | force, trade, persuasion, subterfuge, propaganda |
| flaw | overreach, division, dogma, debt |

## Shape

A group with a power base, a goal it pursues, and a flaw that can be exploited.

## Tagging

[power × goal × method]
