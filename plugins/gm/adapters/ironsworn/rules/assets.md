# Ironsworn Assets — Examples

Assets are abilities, companions, and roles that define your character's approach. Choose three at character creation; you may add more through play. Each asset has three upgradeable abilities — the first is available from the start; you unlock the others by spending experience.

---

## Companion: Cave Lion

*A powerful hunting cat, scarred and loyal.*

- **[1]** When you Secure an Advantage by sending your cave lion to scout ahead, take +1 add.
- **[2]** When you Strike and your cave lion attacks in tandem, inflict +1 harm on a hit.
- **[3]** When your cave lion is at your side and you Face Danger against a physical threat, take +1.

**Health:** [▰▰▰▰] 4/4  
*If the lion is harmed, Endure Harm on its behalf. If health reaches 0, the lion is dead or grievously wounded and cannot act.*

---

## Path: Skirmisher

*You fight light and fast, dancing out of reach.*

- **[1]** When you Strike or Clash using edge, add +1. On a strong hit, take +1 momentum.
- **[2]** When you Face Danger to evade an attack, add +1. On a strong hit, inflict 1 harm in return.
- **[3]** Once per fight, when you Turn the Tide, you may reroll any dice.

---

## Combat Talent: Toughness

*You have learned to take the punishment and keep going.*

- **[1]** When you Endure Harm, take +1. On a strong hit, gain +1 momentum instead of applying the standard choice.
- **[2]** When your health is 3 or lower, take +1 on all action rolls.
- **[3]** When you Face Death, add +1. On a strong hit, return with 3 health.

---

## Path: Ritualist

*You have learned to read the old signs and bargain with the unseen.*

- **[1]** When you Secure an Advantage by performing a ritual at a place of power, take +1 add. On a strong hit, take +2 momentum.
- **[2]** When you Gather Information about a curse, spirit, or ancient site, add +1.
- **[3]** When you Swear an Iron Vow connected to an old power or spirit, add +2 on the roll.

*Note: Rituals in the Ironlands are ambiguous — they may work, or the cost may be hidden. The GM decides whether a ritual site is present and active.*

---

## Companion: Hawk

*A sharp-eyed bird trained to scout and signal.*

- **[1]** When you Gather Information by sending your hawk aloft, add +1. On a strong hit, ask one free question about what the hawk observes.
- **[2]** When you Secure an Advantage before a combat using your hawk's warning, take +2 momentum.
- **[3]** When an enemy attempts to ambush you, your hawk cries warning — you are not caught off guard and may act first.

**Health:** [▰▰] 2/2

---

## Path: Herbalist

*You know the plants that heal, and those that kill.*

- **[1]** When you Sojourn and choose Recuperate, take +1 extra health (total +3 or +1 for companions).
- **[2]** When you Endure Harm and have access to your medicines, add +1.
- **[3]** When you attempt to counteract a poison, disease, or wound on yourself or another, roll +wits. On a hit, treat it as a strong hit for Endure Harm.
