# Starforged Assets (Examples)

Assets represent your starship, modules, paths of expertise, and companions. Each has three abilities; you begin with the first unlocked. Spend experience to unlock additional abilities.

These are representative examples derived from the Starforged SRD. A full campaign should use the complete asset compendium.

---

## Starship asset: Starship

*Your interstellar vessel — the hull that carries you through the Forge. Every Starforged character begins with this asset.*

- [x] **Hull integrity** — your ship has an Integrity track (5 boxes). When your ship takes damage, suffer -Integrity via Withstand Damage.
- [ ] **Armed** — your ship carries weapons. When you Strike or Clash while commanding ship combat, add +1.
- [ ] **Shielded** — your ship has upgraded deflectors. When you React Under Fire in ship combat, add +1.

---

## Module asset: Navigation Array

*An advanced astrogation system for threading the Forge's dangerous passages.*

- [x] **Charted paths** — when you Undertake an Expedition using wits and your destination is known to you, add +1.
- [ ] **Stellar cartography** — when you Gather Information about a star system or route, add +1 and take +1 momentum on a hit.
- [ ] **Emergency jump** — once per expedition, when things go badly wrong in transit, you may burn momentum (reset to 0) to automatically reach the nearest safe haven rather than facing the worst consequences of a miss.

---

## Path asset: Bounty Hunter

*You track targets across the Forge and bring them in — or bring them down.*

- [x] **Pursuit** — when you Gather Information about a wanted target's location or habits, add +1.
- [ ] **Cornered prey** — when you Strike or Clash against a target you have been pursuing for at least one session, take +1 momentum on a hit.
- [ ] **Reputation** — when you Compel someone using your known reputation as leverage (iron), add +1. On a strong hit, take +1 momentum.

---

## Companion asset: Combat Bot

*A rugged, programmable machine that fights at your side.*

- [x] **Combat ready** — your companion adds +1 when you Strike or Clash in a fight where it is present and operational.
- [ ] **Shield protocol** — once per fight, your companion intercepts an attack. On your next React Under Fire, add +1 and suffer 1 less harm.
- [ ] **Adaptive tactics** — when you Secure an Advantage by directing your companion to scout or probe defences, add +1 and take +1 momentum on a hit.

---

## Path asset: Pilot

*You are exceptional at controlling spacecraft under pressure.*

- [x] **Evasive manoeuvres** — when you Face Danger or React Under Fire using edge while piloting your ship, add +1.
- [ ] **Precision approach** — when you Undertake an Expedition by edge (fast and low), on a strong hit take +1 momentum in addition to the normal result.
- [ ] **Redline** — once per expedition leg, you may push the drives past safe limits. Mark +1 progress instead of the normal roll result, then suffer -1 ship Integrity.
